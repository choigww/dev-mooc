Charged Records
Founded 1998
Genre Punk
Country of origin New Jersey, United States

Charged Records was a street punk record label that was formed by Jake Kolatis of The Casualties in 1998. The label released albums from Antidote, The Casualties, The Agrestix, and The Virus, among others. The labeled ceased operations in 2008.[1]
