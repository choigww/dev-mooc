Kelvin Zakayo Mbilinyi (born 24 December 1992), better known by his pseudonym Kevoo Hard, is a Tanzanian musician.[2]

Career[edit]

Mbilinyi is best known for his single Emiwado which was published in 2014. He recorded a single together with Lilian.[3] In his single Love, he combined the languages of Nigeria.[4] He learned the Languages of Nigeria with the help of Nigerian artist and musician Q Chillah (he also appeared in song Love).[4]
