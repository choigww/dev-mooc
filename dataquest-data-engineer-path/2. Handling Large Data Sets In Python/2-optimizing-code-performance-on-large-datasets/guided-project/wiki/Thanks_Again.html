"Thanks Again" is a song written by Jim Rushing and recorded by American country music artist Ricky Skaggs. It was released in June 1988 as the third single from the album Comin' Home to Stay. The song reached #17 on the Billbord Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart.[1]
