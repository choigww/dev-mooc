Furubira in Shiribeshi Subprefecture.

Furubira (古平郡, Furubira-gun?) is a district located in Shiribeshi Subprefecture, Hokkaido, Japan.

As of 2004, the district has an estimated population of 4,055 and a density of 21.52 persons per km². The total area is 188.41 km².
