Aleksandra Katarzyna Jagieło-Przybysz (born 2 June 1980) is a Polish female volleyball player. She was part of the Poland women's national volleyball team at the 2002 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship in Germany.[1] and 2010 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship in Japan.[2]

Clubs[edit]

  • Stal Bielsko-Biala (2002)
  • Bank BPS Muszyna (2010)
