Potheri village, Kanchipuram

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
SRM Lake

Potheri is a town that comes under Maraimalai Nagar municipality of Chengalpattu taluk in Kanchipuram district, Tamil Nadu. It is about 2.5 km from Guduvancheri. SRM University & Valliammai Engineering College are main institutions located here.

The neighbourhood is served by the Potheri railway station Chengalpattu taluk to Chennai Suburban Railway Network.

Transportation[edit]

Potheri town in Chengalpattu taluk located outside Chennai city is well connected by roads and railways, as it is located along the National Highway-45 (NH-45) GST Road, Chennai Tambaram and Chengalpattu Kanchipuram district highway.
