Charles Stuart (rugby union)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
For other uses, see Charles Stuart (disambiguation).

Charles Stuart was a Scottish rugby union player.[1]

He was capped seven times for Scotland between 1909-11.[1] He also played for West of Scotland FC.[1]

He was the brother of Ludovic Stuart who was also capped for Scotland in the 1920s.[1]
