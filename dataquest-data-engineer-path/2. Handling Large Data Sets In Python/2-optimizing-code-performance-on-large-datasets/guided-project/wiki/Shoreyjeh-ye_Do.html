Shoreyjeh-ye Do (Persian: شريجه دو‎‎; also known as Mosherījeh and Moshirijeh)[1] is a village in Esmailiyeh Rural District, in the Central District of Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[2]
