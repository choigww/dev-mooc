Timothy Wanyonyi Wetangula is a Kenyan lawyer and politician who was elected to represent Westlands Constituency in the 2013 election. He is a member of the Orange Democratic Movement and the Coalition for Reforms and Democracy.[1]

Wetangula founded the Kenya Paraplegic Organisation.[1]
