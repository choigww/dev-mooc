Tomohiko Itō
Born (1978-10-20) October 20, 1978 (age 38)
Kasugai, Aichi, Japan
Occupation Director

Tomohiko Itō (伊藤 智彦 Itō Tomohiko; born October 20, 1978) is a Japanese anime director, best known for directing the A-1 Pictures projects Occult Academy, both seasons of Sword Art Online, and Silver Spoon.

Notable works[edit]

TV productions[edit]

  • Death Note (2006) script, storyboard, episode director, Assistant Director
  • Kurozuka (novel) (2008) Storyboard (episode 3), Episode Director (episode 3)
  • Occult Academy (2010) director, storyboard, episode director
  • Fractale (2011) storyboard, episode director
  • Blue Exorcist (2011) storyboard, unit director
  • Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day (2011) storyboard, episode director
  • Guilty Crown (2011/12) Storyboard episodes 3 & 12
  • Sword Art Online (2012) director, storyboard, episode director
  • Silver Spoon (2013) director
  • Sword Art Online II (2014) director, episode director, episode writer
  • Erased (2016) director

Films[edit]

  • The Girl Who Leapt Through Time (2006; assistant director)
  • Piano no Mori (2007; assistant unit director)
  • Summer Wars (2009; assistant director)
  • Sword Art Online The Movie: Ordinal Scale (2017; director, screenwriter)
