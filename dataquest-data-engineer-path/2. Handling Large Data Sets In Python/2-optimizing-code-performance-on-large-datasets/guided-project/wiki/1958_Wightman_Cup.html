1958 Wightman Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The 1958 Wightman Cup was the 30th edition of the annual women's team tennis competition between the United States and Great Britain. It was held at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in London in England in the United Kingdom.[1][2][3]
