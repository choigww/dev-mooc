Plasnewydd electoral ward of Cardiff

Plasnewydd (English: New Manor) is a community and electoral ward of Cardiff, Wales. It falls within the parliamentary constituency of Cardiff Central. It is bounded by the electoral wards of Cyncoed to the north; Penylan to the northeast; Adamsdown to the southwest; and Cathays to the west. It mainly covers the district of Roath. The ward population taken at the 2011 census was 18,166.[1]

Plasnewydd ward elects four councillors to sit on Cardiff Council.[2] The Council's Liberal Democrat leader Rodney Berman represented the Plasnewydd ward until he was defeated, following two recounts, in the May 2012 Council elections.[3] All four seats were then represented by the Labour Party.[2]
