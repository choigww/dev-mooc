Urs Burkart (born January 9, 1963) is a former Swiss professional ice hockey defenceman who last played for Lausanne HC in Switzerland's National League B.

Burkart has participated as a member of the Swiss national team at the 1988 Winter Olympics.[1]

He is currently F&ACE coordinator.
