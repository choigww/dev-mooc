Carte Imagine'R

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The Carte Imagine'R is a travel pass for student and young people between the ages of 12 and 25 years to travel in the Paris Île-de-France region.

Valid for one year, it allows travel on public transport such as Metro, buses, or the RER.

During the week, its user can freely travel in the "zones" defined by its subscription; on weekends, national holidays and during the school holidays, the card "dézonée", authorizing its carrier to travel in all zones of the Paris Île-de-France region.

The card also allows the holder to claim various reductions on other services.

Since 2003 the carte Imagine'R uses the Navigo pass. The validation of the Navigo pass is obligatory with each journey.
