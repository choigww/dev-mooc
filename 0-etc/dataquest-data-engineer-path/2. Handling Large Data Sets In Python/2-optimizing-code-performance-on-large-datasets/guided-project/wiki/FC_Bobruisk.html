FC Bobruisk is a defunct Belarusian football club from Bobruisk.

History[edit]

The team was formed in late 80's as FC Traktor Bobruisk. During Soviet years they played in Belarusian SSR league.

From 1992 until 1995 the team was playing in Belarusian Premier League. In late 1992 they changed name to FC Fandok Bobruisk and 1995 to FC Bobruisk. After unsuccessful 1995 season (they finished last with only 2 points in 15 games) the team was disbanded.

Name changes[edit]

  • Founded as FC Traktor Bobruisk
  • 1992: Renamed to FC Fandok Bobruisk
  • 1995: Renamed to FC Bobruisk

Honours[edit]

  • Belarusian Cup
