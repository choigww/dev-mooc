Terence William Harvey 'Terry' Cox (born 13 March 1937, High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire) played drums in the British folk rock bands Pentangle,[1][2] Duffy's Nucleus and Humblebums.

He also drummed with several other artists, most notably the Bee Gees,[3] David Bowie[4] and Elton John.[5] He was drummer for Charles Aznavour from 1974 till 1982.[citation needed]
