Mirisah (Persian: ميريسه‎‎, also Romanized as Mīrīsah; also known as Marīshe, Merīsah, and Meriseh)[1] is a village in Farmeshkhan Rural District, in the Central District of Kavar County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 209, in 52 families.[2]
