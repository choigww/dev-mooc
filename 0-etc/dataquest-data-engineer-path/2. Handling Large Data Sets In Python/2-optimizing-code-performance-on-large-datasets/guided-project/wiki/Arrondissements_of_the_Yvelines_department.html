Arrondissements of the Yvelines department

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The 4 arrondissements of the Yvelines department are:

  1. Arrondissement of Mantes-la-Jolie, (subprefecture: Mantes-la-Jolie) with 117 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 281,020 in 2013.
  2. Arrondissement of Rambouillet, (subprefecture: Rambouillet) with 81 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 223,370 in 2013.
  3. Arrondissement of Saint-Germain-en-Laye, (subprefecture: Saint-Germain-en-Laye) with 45 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 554,767 in 2013.
  4. Arrondissement of Versailles, (prefecture of the Yvelines department: Versailles) with 19 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 359,327 in 2013.
