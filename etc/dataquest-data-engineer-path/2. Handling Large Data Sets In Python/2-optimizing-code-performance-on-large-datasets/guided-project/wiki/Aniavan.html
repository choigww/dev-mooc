Aniavan (Armenian: Անիավան, formerly Անի կայարանի - Ani kayarani; translates as "Ani station") is a village and rural community (municipality) in the Shirak Province of Armenia. The National Statistical Service of the Republic of Armenia (ARMSTAT) reported its population was 516 in 2010,[1] up from 377 at the 2001 census.[2]
