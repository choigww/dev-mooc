Golabkhvaran (Persian: گلابخواران‎‎, also Romanized as Golābkhvārān; also known as Golābkhvorān)[1] is a village in Tulem Rural District, Tulem District, Sowme'eh Sara County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 112, in 29 families.[2]
