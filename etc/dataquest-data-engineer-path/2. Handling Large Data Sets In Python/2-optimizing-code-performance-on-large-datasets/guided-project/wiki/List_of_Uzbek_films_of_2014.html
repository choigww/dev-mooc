List of Uzbek films of 2014

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The Uzbek film industry produced six feature films in 2014. This article fully lists all non-pornographic films, including short films, that had a release date in that year and which were at least partly made in Uzbekistan. It does not include films first released in previous years that had release dates in 2014.
Also included is an overview of the major events in Uzbek film, including film festivals and awards ceremonies, as well as lists of those films that have been particularly well received, both critically and financially.
