Madhuri Desai
Born Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Occupation Actress
Years active 2013–present

Madhuri Desai is an Indian actress, model and a dancer. She played the lead role of Veda Deshmukh in the Marathi TV serial Yek Number that aired on Star Pravah.[1][2][3]She is currently working with director Mahesh Manjrekar on his upcoming movie alongside other notable stars .[4][5]She has finished her post graduation in Pharma Management from Mumbai University.[6]
