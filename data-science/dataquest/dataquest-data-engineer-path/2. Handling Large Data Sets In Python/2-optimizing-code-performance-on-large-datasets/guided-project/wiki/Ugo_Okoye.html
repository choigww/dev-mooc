Ugochukwu "Ugo" Okoye (born 13 September 1981) is a Nigerian former professional footballer who played as a midfielder.

Career[edit]

Born in Anambra State, Okoye played in the United States for the Charleston Battery, scoring one goals in 34 league appearances during the 2005 and 2006 seasons.[1]
