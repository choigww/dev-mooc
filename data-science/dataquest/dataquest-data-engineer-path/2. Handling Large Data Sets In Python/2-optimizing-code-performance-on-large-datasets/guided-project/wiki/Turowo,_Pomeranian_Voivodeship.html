Turowo [tuˈrɔvɔ] (German: Steinau; Kashubian: Tùrowò) is a village in Gmina Miastko, Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) south-west of Bytów and 62 km (39 mi) south-west of Gdańsk (capital city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship).

From 1975 to 1998 the village was in Słupsk Voivodeship.

It has a population of 124.
