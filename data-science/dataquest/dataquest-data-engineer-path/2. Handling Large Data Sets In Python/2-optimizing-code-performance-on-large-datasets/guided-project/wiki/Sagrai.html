Sagrai is a village in Khandaghosh CD Block in Bardhaman Sadar South subdivision of Bardhaman district in the state of West Bengal, India.

CD Block HQ[edit]

The headquarters of Khandaghosh CD Block are located at Sagrai.[1]

Demographics[edit]

As per the 2011 Census of India Sagrai had a total population of 1,755, of which 877 (50%) were males and 878 (50%) were females. Population below 6 years was 155. The total number of literates in Sagrai was 1,385 (86.56% of the population over 6 years).[2]

Transport[edit]

The State Highway 7 (West Bengal), running from Rajgram (in Murshidabad district) to Midnapore (in (Paschim Medinipur district), passes through Sagrai.[3]
