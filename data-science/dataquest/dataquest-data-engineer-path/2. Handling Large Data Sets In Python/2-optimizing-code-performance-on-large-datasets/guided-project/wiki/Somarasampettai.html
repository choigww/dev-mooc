Somarasampettai is a village in the Srirangam taluk of Tiruchirappalli district in Tamil Nadu, India.

Demographics[edit]

As per the 2001 census, Somarasampettai had a population of 6,270 with 3,167 males and 3,103 females. The sex ratio was 1029 and the literacy rate, 85.26.
