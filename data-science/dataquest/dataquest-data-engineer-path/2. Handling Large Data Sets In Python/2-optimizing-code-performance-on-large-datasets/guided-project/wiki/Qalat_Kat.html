Qalat Kat (Persian: قلات كت‎‎, also Romanized as Qalāt Kat; also known as Qalāt)[1] is a village in Bahmai-ye Garmsiri-ye Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Bahmai County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[2]
