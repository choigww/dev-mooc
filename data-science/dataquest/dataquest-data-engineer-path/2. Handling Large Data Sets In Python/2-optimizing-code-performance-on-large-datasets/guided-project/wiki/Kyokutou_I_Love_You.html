Kyokutou I Love You (極東 I Love You, "Far East I Love You"?) is the 12th album by Buck-Tick, released on March 6, 2002.[1] It reached number twelve on the Oricon chart.[2] "21st Cherry Boy" contains some lyrics from T.Rex's "20th Century Boy, which the song's title is clearly an homage to.
