Zing finger protein 644 (ZNF644) also known as zinc finger motif enhancer-binding protein 2 (Zep-2) is a protein that in humans is encoded by the ZNF644 gene.[3]

Clinical relevance[edit]

Mutations in the ZNF644 gene have been found in sporadic cases of high myopia.[4]
