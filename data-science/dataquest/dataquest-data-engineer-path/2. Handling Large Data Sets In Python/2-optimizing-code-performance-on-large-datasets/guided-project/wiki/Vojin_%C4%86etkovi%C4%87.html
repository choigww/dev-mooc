Vojin Ćetković

Vojin "Vojo" Ćetković (born 22 August 1971 in Kruševac, Serbia, Yugoslavia) is a Serbian actor.

Trivia[edit]

  • His wife is Sloboda Mićalović, a Serbian actress.
  • He is the godfather of two of actor Nebojša Glogovac's sons.
