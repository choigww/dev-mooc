Aldona Orman (born January 1, 1968 in Przedbórz) is a Polish actress.

Aldona Orman was born in Przedbórz, but when she was three months old her family moved to Końskie.[1] There she finished primary school and high school (liceum ogólnokształcące).[1] When Aldona was at high school, she wanted to be a doctor.[2]

After matura Aldona Orman started study at Department of Puppetry at Faculty in Wrocław of Ludwik Solski Academy for the Dramatic Arts. As a student, she made her theatre debut on February 10, 1989 in Polish Theatre in Wrocław. Aldona played Martha Babakin in Ivanov of Anton Chekhov.[3] In 1991 she finished Department of Puppetry at Faculty in Wrocław of Ludwik Solski Academy for the Dramatic Arts.[3]

After the death of her mother Aldona moved to Germany, where her fiancé lived.[1] In this country Orman worked as a theatre actress. She played in Theater Labor, Modernes Theater, Werkstatt Theater and Patos-Transport Theater in Munich.[4]

In 2001 Orman returned to Poland.[2] In 2001 she fall ill of sepsis. After three weeks in hospital she was treated.[2]
