Sobolewo [sɔbɔˈlɛvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Klukowo, within Wysokie Mazowieckie County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Klukowo, 14 km (9 mi) south of Wysokie Mazowieckie, and 58 km (36 mi) south-west of the regional capital Białystok.
