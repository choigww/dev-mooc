Claudia Neidig (born 17 December 1960) is a German stage and film actress.

Neidig studied acting in Karlsruhe from 1977 to 1980. She has appeared in numerous film and television roles. She had a short but memorable role in European Vacation in 1985.

She lives in Bonn, Germany and has a son Claudius.
