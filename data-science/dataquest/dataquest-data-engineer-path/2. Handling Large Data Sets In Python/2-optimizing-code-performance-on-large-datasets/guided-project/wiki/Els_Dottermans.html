Els Dottermans (born 16 May 1964) is a Belgian actress.

Career[edit]

Trained at Studio Herman Teirlinck in Antwerp, Dottermans has been the recipient of several film awards, including two Best Actress wins at the Joseph Plateau Awards,[1] a Golden Calf award for Best Actress at the Nederlands Film Festival, and a Golden Goblet award for Best Actress at the Shanghai International Film Festival.[2][3]

Personal life[edit]

Dottermans is married with two children.
