The EDP Sarichioi Wind Farm is a proposed wind power project in Sarichioi, Tulcea County, Romania. It will have 11 individual Vestas V90 wind turbines[1] with a nominal output of around 3 MW which will deliver up to 33 MW of power, enough to power over 21,500 homes, with a capital investment required of approximately US$50 million.
