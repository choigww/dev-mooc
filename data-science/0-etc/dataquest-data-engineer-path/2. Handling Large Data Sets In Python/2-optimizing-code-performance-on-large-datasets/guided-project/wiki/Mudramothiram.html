Mudramothiram is a 1978 Indian Malayalam film, directed by J. Sasikumar and produced by EK Thyagarajan. The film stars Prem Nazir, Jayabharathi, Jagathy Sreekumar and Sankaradi in lead roles. The film had musical score by G. Devarajan.[1][2][3]
