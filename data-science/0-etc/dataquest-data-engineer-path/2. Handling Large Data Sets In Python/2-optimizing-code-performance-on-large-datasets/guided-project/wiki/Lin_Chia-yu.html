Lin Chia-yu (Chinese: 林家佑; pinyin: Línjiā Yòu; Wade–Giles: Lin Chia-yu; born 30 June 1993) is a Taiwanese male badminton player.[1] In 2011, he won the gold medal at the Asian Junior Championships in men's doubles event partnered with Huang Po-jui.[2] He also won the silver medal at the BWF World Junior Championships.[3]
