Punjab War

Topographical map of The Punjab, "Land of 5 Waters"
Date 1810 - 1820
Location Punjab
Result Sikh victory
Belligerents
Sikh Empire

 British Empire

  • British East India Company

The Punjab War was fought from 1810 to 1820, between the Sikh Empire and the British East India Company. The Sikh Empire claimed victory.
