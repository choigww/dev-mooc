The Curtiss-Wright Hangar, also known as Owens Field Municipal Airport Hangar, is a historic hangar located at Jim Hamilton – L.B. Owens Airport, Columbia, South Carolina. Built in 1929 by Curtiss-Wright, it consists of a central metal-clad barrel roofed storage area flanked on either side by flat-roofed wings.[2][3]

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1998.[1]
