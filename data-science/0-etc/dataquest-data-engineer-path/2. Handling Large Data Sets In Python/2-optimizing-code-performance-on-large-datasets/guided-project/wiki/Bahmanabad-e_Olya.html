Bahmanabad-e Olya (Persian: بهمن آباد عليا‎‎, also Romanized as Bahmanābād-e ‘Olyā; also known as Rashnowābād)[1] is a village in Zarrin Dasht Rural District, in the Central District of Darreh Shahr County, Ilam Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 248, in 54 families.[2]
