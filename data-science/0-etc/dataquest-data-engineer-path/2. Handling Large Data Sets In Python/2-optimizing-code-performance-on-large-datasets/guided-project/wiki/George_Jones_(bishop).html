George Jones (bishop)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
  • Anglicanism portal

The Right Reverend George Lewis Jones (died 9 March 1804), was Bishop of Kilmore from 1775 to 1790[1] when he was translated to Kildare.[2]

He died on 9 March 1804.[3]
