"When You Loved Me" is a song written by Joy Byers and Bob Tubert and performed by Brenda Lee.[1] The song reached #8 on the adult contemporary chart and #47 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1964.[2] It was featured on her 1965 album, Brenda Lee Sings Top Teen Hits.[3]

The single's B-side, "He's Sure to Remember Me", reached #135 on the Billboard chart.[4]
