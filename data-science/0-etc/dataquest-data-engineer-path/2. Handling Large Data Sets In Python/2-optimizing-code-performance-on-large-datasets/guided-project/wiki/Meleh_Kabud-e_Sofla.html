Meleh Kabud-e Sofla (Persian: مله كبود سفلي‎‎, also Romanized as Meleh Kabūd-e Soflá)[1] is a village in Kakavand-e Sharqi Rural District, Kakavand District, Delfan County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 11 families.[2]
