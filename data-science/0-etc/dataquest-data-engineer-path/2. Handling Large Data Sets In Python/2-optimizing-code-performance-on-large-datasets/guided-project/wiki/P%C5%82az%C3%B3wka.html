Płazówka [pwaˈzufka] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dzikowiec, within Kolbuszowa County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north of Dzikowiec, 9 km (6 mi) north-east of Kolbuszowa, and 34 km (21 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszów.
