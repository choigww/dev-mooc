Central District (Rezvanshahr County)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The Central District of Rezvanshahr County (Persian: بخش مرکزی شهرستان رضوان‌شهر‎‎) is a district (bakhsh) in Rezvanshahr County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 34,160, in 8,902 families.[1] The District has one city: Rezvanshahr. The District has two rural districts (dehestan): Gil Dulab Rural District and Khoshabar Rural District.
