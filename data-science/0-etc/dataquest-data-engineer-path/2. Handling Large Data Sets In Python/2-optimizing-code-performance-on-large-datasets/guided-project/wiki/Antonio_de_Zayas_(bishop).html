Antonio de Zayas (died 16 Oct 1582) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Nicaragua (1575–1582).[1][2][3][4]

Biography[edit]

Antonio de Zayas was from Ecija, Spain and ordained a priest in the Order of Friars Minor.[1] On 19 Jan 1575, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Gregory XIII as Bishop of Nicaragua[1] and arrived in Nicaragua in January 1576.[4] Suffering from ill health,[4] he resigned as Bishop of Nicaragua on 8 Mar 1582 and died on 16 Oct 1582.[1]
