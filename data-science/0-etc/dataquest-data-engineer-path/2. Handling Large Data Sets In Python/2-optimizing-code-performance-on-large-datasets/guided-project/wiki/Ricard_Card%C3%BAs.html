Ricard Cardús González (born 18 March 1988) is a Grand Prix motorcycle racer from Spain.[1] He is the nephew of Carlos Cardús. He is a former competitor of the Spanish 125GP Championship, the Spanish Supersport Championship and the Spanish Moto2 series. He is currently the test rider for the KTM Moto2 project.
